# Projects & Activities

## Stanford Projects
- MealMatch: Designed a mobile app to help students plan spontaneous meals with friends, focusing on time constraints, coordination, and making social dining seamless
- Caltrain Discovery Project: Created a swipe-based interface for riders to discover local businesses and attractions, designed to boost tourism and support small businesses
- Cultural Trends in Grammy Winners (ML + NLP Project): Built and managed machine learning model in Python (using Pandas, Scikit-learn, VADER) to analyze 60+ years of Grammy Records data, performing sentiment analysis, TF-IDF vectorization and KMeans clustering to identify lyrical and cultural trends

## Leadership & Activities

- VMware Women's Leadership Innovation Lab (Undergraduate Leader): Deliver a 9-module leadership curriculum on negotiation and entrepreneurship to high school students, mentoring them to build confidence and problem-solving skills
